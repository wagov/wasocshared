Reporting

Agencies whose cyber security teams have enrolled in the DGov Incident Reporting Portal (IRP) should raise an incident within the portal.

The Cyber Security Unit will liaise with the Australian Cyber Security Centre and other States and Territories in support of nation-wide incident coordination.
