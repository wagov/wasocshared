# Chrome Multiple Critical Vulnerabilities - 20260608001

## Overview

The WASOC has been made aware of a Google Chrome update with 429 security fixes that covers a wide range of vulnerabilities, including several rated as critical. Such as a sandbox escape via a crafted HTML page, impacting a range of desktop platforms.

## What is vulnerable?

| Product(s) Affected | Version(s)             | CVE                                                                                                                                                                                                             | CVSS                  | Severity                                         |
| ------------------- | ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| Google Chrome       | prior to 149.0.7827.53 | [CVE-2026-11250](https://nvd.nist.gov/vuln/detail/CVE-2026-11250) <br> [CVE-2026-11282](https://nvd.nist.gov/vuln/detail/CVE-2026-11282) <br> [CVE-2026-11293](https://nvd.nist.gov/vuln/detail/CVE-2026-11293) | 9.6 <br> 9.6 <br> 9.6 | **Critical** <br> **Critical** <br> **Critical** |

## What has been observed?

The WASOC has not received any reports of exploitation of this vulnerability on Western Australian Government networks at the time of writing.

## Recommendation

The WASOC recommends administrators apply the solutions as per vendor instructions to all affected devices within expected timeframes (refer [Patch Management](../guidelines/patch-management.md)):
